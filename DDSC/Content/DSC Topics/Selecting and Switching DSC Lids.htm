The information below details how to select the appropriate Discovery DSC lid for your experiment. Also included are details on the correct process of switching the lids.

Selecting the Appropriate Lid for a Discovery DSC Experiment

  • DANGER: The cell surfaces can be hot enough to burn the skin during a sample run. If you are conducting a subambient test on the DSC, cold surfaces can also cause injury. After running any type of experiment, you must allow the DSC AutoLid to return to the standby temperature before installing or replacing the lid.

Choosing the appropriate lid is an important part of running a successful Discovery DSC experiment. See the information below.

The Discovery DSC is shipped with two lids:a coated lid for operation up to 400°C, and a high temperature silver lid for operation up to 725°C. After unpacking the instrument and installing the instrument completely as described in the Discovery DSC Getting Started Guide, you are ready to install a lid and run samples.

 

  • NOTE: After the silver lid is used for high-temperature experiments, the cell should be conditioned using a cell conditioning tool (shown below) before reverting to the coated lid for experiments. The tool is included in the Cell Conditioning Tool Kit, which is available for separate purchase. For more information, see Using the Discovery DSC Cell Conditioning Tool Kit.

When you receive your Discovery DSC instrument, the lids are shipped in an accessory box, separate from the instrument. Follow the instructions in the next section to install the lid.

Installing the Lid

To install the lid on the Discovery DSC:

  1. Ensure that the instrument is not running a method and that the AutoLid is at a safe temperature to handle.
  2. Select the lid you wish to install and locate the notch on the top side of the lid. See the figure below.

  1. Gently grasp the sides of the AutoLid cover and slowly lift it to expose more of the lid pin.

  1. While still holding the AutoLid cover, install the lid by aligning the notch on the top side of the lid with the lid pin.

  1. Specify the installed lid type through the Discovery DSC > General page of TRIOS Options.

Conditioning the Cell

Clean (condition) the cell:

  • If you notice changes in the baseline
  • If you changed the lids
  • Before T zero calibration

For more information, see Using the Discovery DSC Cell Conditioning Tool Kit.
