Drying the DSC-RCS or DSC-LN2P System

Under certain conditions, it may be necessary to dry out the DSC-RCS or DSC-LN2P system. To remove moisture in the DSC cell and cooling head, follow the instructions below.

  1. Verify that the DSC cell is empty and close the lid. Verify that the AutoLid is seated properly.
  2. Access the TRIOS Options > Discovery DSC > Cooler page of TRIOS software. Verify that the correct cooler type (RCS 120, RCS 40, RCS 90, or LN2P) is selected.
  3. Check Leave Cooler On.
  4. Check Activate secondary purge when lid is opened (RCS and LN2P), if desired. With this option selected, the purge automatically turns on whenever the cell is opened by the AutoLid.

  1. Access the TRIOS Options > Discovery DSC > Auto Sampler page of TRIOS software and select Leave Cooler On as a Sequence Stop Option. This allows the RCS or LN2P event function to operate.

  1. Access the TRIOS Control panel > General page and verify the desired Standby Temperature.
  2. Access the Experiment Procedure section to create a custom method.
  3. Using the instructions found in Creating Custom Methods, set up the following method:

Event1 Off (for RCS) or LNC Control Off (for LN2P)

Equilibrate 75.00°C

Air Cool On

Isothermal 120.0 min (or less depending upon the application)

Air Cool Off

Event1 On (for RCS) or LNC Control Off (for LN2P)

These conditions are suitable for typical situations.

  1. In the Experiment Procedure section:
  2. Name the method Cell Cooler Conditioning (or similar) and save the method for future recall.
  3. Click Advanced to display the End of Test options. Check Use Standby Temperature. Once the RCS or LN2P is operating, it is very important that the cell is always kept slightly above ambient temperature before and after experiments.

  1. Start the experiment.
  1. Upon completion of this experiment, it is recommended that the base and cell purges remain on continuously. If the purges do not remain on, atmospheric moisture may contaminate the system and, depending on the time involved and relative humidity, the procedure may have to be repeated. The base purge is automatically active when the RCS or LN2P is selected as the cooler type.
