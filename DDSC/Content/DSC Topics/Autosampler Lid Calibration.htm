In rare cases, it may be necessary to calibrate the AutoLid. Calibration of the lid ensures that the lift rod, inner lid, and outer lid sit properly atop the cell.

Preparing the AutoLid for Calibration

Before performing a calibration, perform the following steps:

  1. Ensure that the Discovery DSC components are at a safe handling temperature.
  2. Open the lid.
  3. Remove any pans from the cell.
  4. Remove the AutoLid arm cover by simultaneously pulling out and lifting the sides of the cover in the locations and order shown below.

  1. From the Discovery DSC user interface, access the Autosampler calibration functions by selecting Set View > Autosampler > Calibrate.
  2. Select Options > and press Lid Calibration. The following screen appears.

  1. Once the AutoLid arm is prepped for calibration, press Start and proceed to the next section, Aligning (Calibrating) the Lid.

Aligning (Calibrating) the Lid

After preparing the AutoLid arm for lid calibration (as described in the previous section), follow the instructions below:

  1. From the Lid Calibration screen, press Center. The AutoLid arm moves slightly above the cell position.

  2. Make sure the lid is centered with the cell by turning the adjustment screws.

  1. Press Height. The AutoLid arm moves to the closed position in order to adjust the height of the lid on the cell

  1. Use the adjustment arrows to lower the inner lid onto the cell so that the lift rod lip is no longer touching the inner lid notch. The lift rod lip should be about halfway between the top of the inner lid notch and the inner lid base, as shown below. Also, ensure that the sides of the inner lid are centered on the cell.

  1. When satisfied with the calibration, press Save.
  2. The calibration confirmation screen appears. Press OK.
  3. Test by opening and closing the lid and checking the lift rod's position.
  4. To exit Lid Calibration, press Exit to return to the user interface main menu, or press < Back to return to the calibrations options screen.

Restoring the Discovery DSC to Operating Condition

Restoring the Discovery DSC to operating condition requires the following steps:

  1. Move the lid to the open position.
  2. Remove the inner lid.
  3. Reinstall the outer cover (heat shield). Secure the outer cover to the AutoLid arm using the three screws circled below.

 

  1. Replace the inner lid.
  2. Reinstall the AutoLid arm cover by pulling out the sides of the cover while placing it over the AutoLid arm.
  3. NOTE: The AutoLid arm cover will "snap" into place when installed properly.
