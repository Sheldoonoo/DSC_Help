Discovery DSC: Preparing High Volume Pans

The high volume pans require the use of the yellow die set shown in the figure below. There is one lower yellow die (right) and one upper yellow die (left).

For additional information on these pans, refer to the High Volume Pan Kit Getting Started Guide.

 

Follow these basic instructions to use the Tzero sample press and the high volume pans:

  1. Select the desired sample pan, matching lid, and o-ring, and obtain the yellow die set.

 

  1. NOTE: Always handle the pans and lids carefully with tweezers. Ensure that all surfaces that the pans are placed on are clean and free of any possible contaminants.
  2. Weigh the empty pan, o-ring, and lid.
  3. Use tweezers to place the o-ring in the lid and push it all the way to the top of the lid.
  4. Prepare the sample and carefully place the sample in the pan. If you are using a powder or granular sample, spread it evenly in the pan. Do not allow the sample to spill onto the lip of the pan.
  5. Weigh the filled sample pan and lid/o-ring and determine the sample weight. It is important to control the amount of sample you are using. Refer to additional material in the training courses for optimal sample size considerations.
  6. Place the filled sample pan in the lower die and position the matching lid in place.
  7. Push the press handle back and place the upper yellow die into the press. The upper die slides in easily and a magnet will hold it in place.
  8. Place the lower yellow die with the prepared sample pan and lid into the press. Position the die with the raised platform to the rear of the press as shown in the figure below. Rotate the die slightly to ensure that it is seated properly.

  1. Pull the handle forward until it stops to push the lid onto the pan.
  2. CAUTION: Make sure that you hold the handle firmly throughout the entire pulling procedure!
  3. Push the handle back again and remove the lower die and pan.
  4. Prepare an empty pan and lid for use as the reference pan. It is important that the same care be taken in preparing the reference pan as in preparing the sample pan.
