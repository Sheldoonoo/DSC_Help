Desmear

The Desmear window allows you to improve the resolution of sharp melting peaks. The desmearing routine is an offline approach and requires you to adjust a value until the desired curve shape is obtained. To desmear a curve:

  1. Open the desired data file.
  2. Right-click the curve on the graph and click Desmear...

  1. The Desmear dialog box displays. Enter the desired Time constant(s) and the desired Derivative window(s).

  1. Click OK when finished.
