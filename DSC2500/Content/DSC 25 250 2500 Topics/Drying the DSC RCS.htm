Drying the DSC-RCS System

Under certain conditions, it may be necessary to dry out the DSC-RCS system. To remove moisture in the DSC cell and cooling head, follow the instructions below.

  1. Verify that the DSC cell is empty and close the lid. Verify that the lid is seated properly.
  2. Access the TRIOS Options > Discovery DSC > Cooler page of TRIOS software. Verify that the correct cooler type (RCS 120, RCS 40, RCS 90) is selected.
  • Make sure that the RCS is in Event mode.

  1. Check Activate secondary purge when lid is opened (RCS), if desired. With this option selected, the purge automatically turns on whenever the Autosampler lid is opened.
  1. Access the TRIOS Control panel > General page and verify the desired Standby Temperature.
  2. Access the Experiment Procedure section to create a custom method.
  3. Set up the below method using the Cell Conditioning Test template or the instructions found in Creating Custom Methods:

  2. Name the method Cell Cooler Conditioning (or similar) and save the method for future recall.
  3. Click Advanced to display the End of Test options. Check Use Standby Temperature. Once the RCS is operating, it is very important that the cell is always kept slightly above ambient temperature before and after experiments.
  1. Start the experiment.
  1. Upon completion of this experiment, it is recommended that the base and cell purges remain on continuously. If the purges do not remain on, atmospheric moisture may contaminate the system and, depending on the time involved and relative humidity, the procedure may have to be repeated. The base purge is automatically active when the RCS is selected as the cooler type.
