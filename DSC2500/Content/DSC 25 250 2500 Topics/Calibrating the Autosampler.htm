Preparing the AutoLid for Calibration

Before performing a calibration, perform the following steps:

  1. Ensure that the Discovery DSC components are at a safe handling temperature.
  2. Open the lid.
  3. Remove any pans from the cell.
  4. Remove the AutoLid arm cover by simultaneously pulling out the right edge of the cover, rotating it and lifting it up.

 

  1. From the Discovery DSC user interface, access the Autosampler calibration functions by selecting  Autosampler > Calibration .
  2. Press Lid Open.
  3. Remove the inner lid by lifting it up and sliding it off of the lift rod.

  1. To remove the outer lid (heat shield), loosen the three screws circled in the figure below.

  1. Replace the inner lid.
  2. Use the adjustment arrows to lower the inner lid onto the cell so that the lift rod lip is no longer touching the inner lid notch. The lift rod lip should be about halfway between the top of the inner lid notch and the inner lid base, as shown below. Also, ensure that the sides of the inner lid are centered on the cell.

  1. When satisfied with the calibration, press Save.
  2. Test by opening and closing the lid and checking the lift rod's position.

Perform the Calibration (Verification and Adjustment Procedure)

In the event that the Autosampler drops a pan or you receive an Autosampler error, it may necessary to refine your Autosampler calibration. In most cases, no other adjustments or calibrations are necessary.

To begin verification and adjustment routine, follow these steps:

  1. From the Discovery DSC user interface main screen, access the Autosampler calibration functions by selecting  Autosampler > Calibration .

 

  1. Remove any pans from tray 1.
  2. Place a clear sapphire disk in sample tray position 1.
  3. From the user interface, press an item in the list to choose the calibration to perform. Refer to the table below for information regarding the individual calibrations.

  1. The Autosampler moves to the specified location for verification. If the position is acceptable, select Yes to continue. Otherwise, select Adjust to adjust.
  • If you select Continue, the sapphire disk moves to the next position in the verification sequence.
  • If you select Adjust, the adjustment window displays, allowing for manual adjustment (see Using the Autosampler Adjustment Window for further details).Once you are satisfied with the adjustments, select Save and press Continue to return to step 4.
  1. The process repeats until all the positions are verified.

Using the Autosampler Adjustment Window

The Autosampler adjustment screen allows you to make adjustments to the arm laterally. Adjustments from this screen are applied to the selected position(s) only.

 

  1. Move the Autosampler to the desired position, as well as the following:
  • Use the Close Grip and Open Grip functions to grip and release the pan.
  • Change the increment in which the Autosampler adjusts by selecting a value from the slider.
  1. Press Check to verify the position.
  2. Once the position is refined, press Save.

Restoring the Discovery DSC to Operating Condition

Restoring the Discovery DSC to operating condition requires the following steps:

  1. Move the lid to the open position.
  2. Remove the inner lid by lifting it up and sliding it off of the lift rod.

  1. Reinstall the outer cover (heat shield). Secure the outer cover to the AutoLid arm using the three screws circled below.

 

  1. Replace the inner lid.
  2. Reinstall the AutoLid arm cover by pulling out the sides of the cover while placing it over the AutoLid arm.
  3. NOTE: The AutoLid arm cover will "snap" into place when installed properly.
