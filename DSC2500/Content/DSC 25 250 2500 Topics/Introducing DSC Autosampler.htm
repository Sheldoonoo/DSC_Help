Introducing the Discovery DSC Autosampler

The DSC Autosampler (shown below) is standard on the TA Instruments Discovery DSC 2500 and optional for the Discovery DSC 250 and 250.

The Autosampler is a four-axis robotic device that automatically loads sample and reference pans between the DSC measurement cell and the Autosampler tray or load position. Once the Autosampler loads the pans and the cell covers are in place, the controller takes over and runs the pre-programmed thermal analysis experiments. After the experiment is complete and the cell returns to standby temperature, the instrument uncovers the cell and the Autosampler then unloads the pans. The unattended process can be pre- programmed to run up to 53 samples with one reference pan.
