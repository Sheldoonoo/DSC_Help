Discovery DSC: Preparing Tzero Aluminum Pans and Tzero Low-Mass Aluminum Pans

The TzeroÂ® aluminum pans require the use of the black die set shown in the figure below.

There is one lower black die (larger diameter) and two upper black dies. Only one of these two upper dies is used for a given sample.

  • The upper black die with a flat surface is used for samples that lie flat and evenly in the sample pan. It will maximize the contact of the lid and sample.
  • The upper black die with the cupped surface is used for samples that are irregularly shaped. This die will conform the lid to the irregular sample and minimize distortion of the bottom of the sample pan.

To prepare a sample in a Tzero aluminum pan, consider the comments above and follow the instructions in Using the Tzero Press.
