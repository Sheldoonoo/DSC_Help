Discovery DSC: Preparing Standard Aluminum Pans

The standard aluminum pans require the use of the green die set shown in the figure below.

There is one lower green die (larger diameter) and two upper green dies. Only one of these two upper dies is used for a given sample.

  • The upper green die with a flat surface is used for samples that lie flat and evenly in the sample pan. It will maximize the contact of the lid and sample.
  • The upper green die with the concave surface is used for samples that are irregularly shaped. This die will conform the lid to the irregular sample and minimize distortion of the bottom of the sample pan.

To prepare a sample in a Standard aluminum pan, consider the comments above and follow the instructions in Using the Tzero Press.
