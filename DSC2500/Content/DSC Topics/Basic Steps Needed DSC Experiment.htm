Basic Steps Needed to Start a DSC Experiment

All of your DSC experiments have the following general outline. In some cases, not all of these steps are performed.

  1. Calibration/verification of the instrument performance.
  2. Selecting the sample pan type.
  3. Selecting and preparing a sample.
  4. Loading the sample into the pan and loading the pan into the cell or Autosampler tray.
  5. Setting up your experiment through the TRIOS software.
  6. Starting the experiment and/or run sequence.
